About Us

Building the Future, One Coder at a Time

At NanoCoders, we believe the future belongs to those who start young. We're a youth-led educational tech startup based in Peshawar, Pakistan, dedicated to teaching coding, game development, web design, app creation, and digital creativity to children and teenagers — through fun, engaging, and project-based learning.

Founded by Muhammad Ziyam Khan, a 16-year-old tech enthusiast, game developer, and entrepreneur, NanoCoders is built on the belief that kids don't need to wait to become tech leaders — they can start now.

Our Mission

To make coding and digital creativity accessible, fun, and empowering for kids and teens — especially in underserved communities across Pakistan.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's leading platform for coding education for kids, and to build a generation that's not just consuming technology — but creating it.
